

# LifeBox NextGen Pvt. Ltd. — Corporate Website Plan

## Design System

- **Color Palette**: Deep navy primary (#0F172A), electric blue accent (#3B82F6), clean whites, subtle grays — enterprise SaaS aesthetic
- **Typography**: Inter for body, bold weights for headings — clean, professional, highly readable
- **Spacing**: 8px grid system with generous whitespace, structured sections
- **Style**: Minimal animations (subtle fade-ins only), no flashy effects, strong visual hierarchy

---

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero Section**: Bold headline "Building Intelligent Digital Infrastructure for Modern Enterprises" with subheadline, two CTAs (Explore Products / Request Consultation), and a mock product dashboard illustration
- **Products Overview**: Grid of 5 product cards (NextGenFreeEdu, Gnan AI, LifeOS, ERP, CRM) with icons and one-line descriptions
- **Services Snapshot**: 5 service categories in structured cards
- **Trust Bar**: MSME registration badge, key stats (products built, clients served, uptime), security/compliance mentions
- **Testimonials**: Rotating quotes from enterprise clients (placeholder content)
- **CTA Section**: "Ready to transform your operations?" with consultation request button

### 2. About Us
- Company mission and vision
- Company structure overview
- MSME registration and compliance credentials
- Leadership team section (placeholder)
- Timeline/milestones

### 3. Products Page
- Overview grid of all 5 products
- Each product gets a detailed section with: overview, core features list, technical architecture summary, target users, business benefits
- **Gnan AI** gets premium positioning: AI mock interviews, resume evaluation, coding auto-grading, performance analytics, interview readiness score — clearly positioned as "AI-Powered Interview & Placement Preparation Platform"
- Product dashboard mockup illustrations for each

### 4. Services Page
- Five service categories (Enterprise Solutions, Engineering, Cloud & Infrastructure, Design & Branding, Media & Growth)
- Each service includes: problem statement, solution approach, technologies used, outcome
- Enterprise-focused language throughout
- "From idea validation to production deployment" positioning

### 5. Case Studies
- Structured case study cards with industry, challenge, solution, outcome
- Placeholder content for 3-4 case studies across education, enterprise, and AI verticals
- Filterable by industry/service type

### 6. Industries Served
- Visual grid: Education, Healthcare, Finance, Retail, Government, Manufacturing
- Each with a brief description of how LifeBox serves that vertical

### 7. Technology Stack
- Visual display of technologies organized by category (Frontend, Backend, Cloud, AI/ML, DevOps)
- Architecture philosophy section
- Security & compliance emphasis

### 8. Careers
- Company culture section
- Open positions listing (placeholder)
- Application form or CTA

### 9. Contact
- Validated contact form (name, email, company, message with Zod validation)
- Office location details
- Business inquiry categories dropdown

---

## Global Components
- **Navbar**: Logo, page links, "Request Consultation" CTA button — sticky, clean
- **Footer**: Company info, quick links, social links, MSME mention, copyright
- **SEO**: Semantic HTML, structured metadata per page, Open Graph tags, page titles/descriptions

---

## Key Principles
- No hype, no startup gimmicks — confident enterprise tone
- Every section structured with clear visual hierarchy
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- All product/service content uses the exact details provided
- Accessible, performant, production-ready
